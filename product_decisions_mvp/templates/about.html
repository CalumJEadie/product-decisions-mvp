About

Our mission


21 Spokes wants to get more people riding great bikes.

We're helping commuters all over London discover the best second-hand bikes available and serve them with personal recommendations.

  • We make it easy to find a bike that fits your size and personality.
  • Online marketplaces can be daunting, our curated selection cuts through the noise.
  • And if nothing feels quite right, drop us an email and we'll provide a more tailored recommendation.

Who are we?


21 Spokes was started by Calum and Andrew. They were helping a couple of friends choose bikes and ended up bombarding them with link after link of interesting finds from eBay. They were trawling through pages of listings to find second-hand gems to recommend.

With a few happy friends now cycling, they want to help others discover used bikes with a bit of character and personality. They're usually cheaper too. So this is our first attempt at bringing friendly recommendations to the masses.

Contact us through this site. We love helping customers find the perfect bike or going for coffee.
